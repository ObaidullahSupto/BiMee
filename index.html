About Us

We are trying to digitize the education process.

Our goal is to digitize the education process and create a helping aid for teachers to teach students more efficiently through the use of Artificial Intelligence. In addition, we aim to build a stronger workforce for the ICT sector by training the students in Universities on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We are developing a software to enable the transformation of the traditional education system to a digital model. Furthermore, we teach programming to children through a curriculum which was made using the MIT-created program Scratch, in partnership with STEMON.

Our Services

In Bangladesh due to an inefficient educational system many brilliant minds are being misdirected. Thus, we aim to leverage the strengths of emerging technologies such as AI and ML to fill this gap in the market and provide a solution for smart education that will improve the efficiency of the current system.
